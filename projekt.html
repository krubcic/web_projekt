What is Dragon Ball?

Dragon Ball is a Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Akira Toriyama. It follows the adventures of Goku and his friends as they search for the Dragon Balls, which can grant any wish. The series is very influential and has been adapted into many anime and video games. It is known for its action-packed battles and colorful characters. The story of Dragon Ball follows Goku and his friends as they travel the world searching for the Dragon Balls, which are seven magical orbs scattered throughout the world. Along the way, they encounter a wide range of enemies and allies and must use their fighting skills to defend the Earth from powerful threats. The series is known for its action-packed battles and character development.
